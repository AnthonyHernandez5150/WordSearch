# WordTrail Play Store Draft

## App Name

WordTrail

## Short Description

A polished word-search game with daily streaks, shaped boards, and satisfying swipe-to-find puzzles.

## Full Description

WordTrail is a modern word-search puzzle game built for quick daily play and focused brain-training sessions.

Trace words across clean letter boards, follow glowing paths, and build a daily streak as you clear puzzles. Choose a relaxed Calm board, a balanced Explorer board, or a tougher Expert board with trickier word placement and decoys.

Features:

- Classic word-search boards
- Shaped boards like hearts, diamonds, trees, moons, and butterflies
- Calm, Explorer, and Expert difficulty options
- Swipe-to-select word tracing
- Found-word bursts and satisfying board-complete rewards
- Daily streaks, clean streaks, best times, and saved progress
- Offline-first play with no account required

WordTrail is designed to feel calm, readable, and polished while still giving puzzle fans something to chase.

## Suggested Closed-Test Notes

Thanks for testing WordTrail. Please try:

- Completing one Calm board
- Completing one Expert board
- Trying at least one Shaped board
- Closing and reopening the app to confirm streaks stay saved
- Reporting any board that feels too easy, too hard, or hard to read
